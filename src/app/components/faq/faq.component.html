FAQ

What is BeSafe?

BeSafe is an application which can help minimise loss of life and chances of prolonged injury due to accidents in secluded places. It also indicates the nearby accident prone areas based on our geographical location.

How can it help?

BeSafe indicates the user by sending him or her a notification as soon as he enters an accident prone zone.

How much it costs?

Using BeSafe is free. Value of Human Life is more than Money.
